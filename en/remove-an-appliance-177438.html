Remove an appliance

To remove an appliance from your environment, you must deprovision the appliance from the Netskope tenant and then delete the appliance from the tenant UI.

Step 1: Deprovision the appliance

To deprovison the appliance from the Netskope tenant, at the nsshell prompt run the command,

deprovision
Step 2: Delete the appliance

To delete the appliance from the tenant UI,

  1. Log into the Netskope tenant UI and navigate to Settings > Security Cloud Platform > On Premises > On-Premises Infrastructure.

  2. On the On-Premises Infrastructure page, scroll down to the Infrastructure section. Locate the deprovisioned appliance you want to delete and click the delete icon, .
