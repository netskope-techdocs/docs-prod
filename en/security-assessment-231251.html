Security Assessment

Netskope performs Continuous Security Assessment scans on your public cloud accounts configured in the Netskope tenant. The scans provide visibility into your IaaS resources and their security posture. The Inventory section of Netskope's Cloud Infrastructure page provides details on several resource categories.

The resources in the public cloud accounts that are defined in the tenant are audited for being compliant with Industry standard or best practices. You can view the compliance findings in the Compliance section of Netskope's Cloud Infrastructure page.

Netskope begins building an inventory within five minutes of the account configuration. Subsequently, inventory updates are run at the same frequency as the compliance assessments. At the time of the account setup, you can choose to run the compliance assessments every 30 minutes, 60 minutes, 2 hours, 6 hours, or 24 hours.
